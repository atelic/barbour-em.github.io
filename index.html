About

Education

I am currently a second-year undergraduate student at The University of Virginia studying to obtain a Bachelors in Compputer Science, as well as a Bachelors in Arabic Language and Literature. As such, I am captivated by both the minute forces that shape and and drive businesses, as well and the linguistic forces that drive culture.

Interests

I'm interested web and software development but when I'm not coding I'm hiking, camping, or kayaking. I am beginning to seek positions in which I can apply my development and programming skills as well as my background with Arabic. I hope to use my unique set of skills and experience to bring value to a business, small or large, to facilitate strategic growth and development.

Skills and technologies

I am a passionate, self-taught full stack developer. I have experience in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, PHP, Ruby, and Python. I am also familiar with recent practices such as daily usage of Sass, task-runners such as Gulp, and version control with Git. I also have experience with Laravel and CakePHP frameworks, as well as JavaScript Frameworks such as AngularJS. Check out some of my projects below!

AngularJS Scheduling App

AngularJS is an awesome and popular JavaScript framework. Here is the first thing I built using Angular; it's a real-time scheduling app that uses Firebase, an API that can store and sync real-time data.

RigidCSS

Building respoinsive, mobile-first sites almost always requires a responsive grid system. I got tired of using bloated frameworks like Bootstrap and Foundation so I built my own. It's a simple, percentage based grid that allows you to focus on the content and styling first and setting simple class names to let the grid do its thing.
